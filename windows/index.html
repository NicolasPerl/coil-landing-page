Coil points are awarded to users when certain actions are taken on the coil platform. Coil points will be class specific meaning a student could have 0 points in history, 80 points in physics, and 30 points in math. Each user will also have their total coil points displayed on their profile. In this example, the student would have 110 total coil points

How to get points

You can accrue points many different ways!
  • When a student creates a study group -- 20 points
  • When a student joins a study group -- 10 points
  • When a student requests 1 on 1 help -- 5 points
  • When a requestor closes a session and rates the tutor -- 40 points to the tutor
  • When a student posts in the class - 1 point
